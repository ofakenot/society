Frequently asked

What is BRX Society?

BRX is an exclusive members club for those who strive for personal greatness.Its culture extends beyond business—encouraging members to live their lives to a full extent. Here, the the next A-players gather to work, plan, teach, and inspire—bringing each other to even higher levels.

How to join

Step 1 – Apply to the waitlist
‍Step 2 – Get pre-approved
‍Step 3 – Intro call with Head of Memberships
‍Step 4 – Get officially invited
‍Step 5 – Book your qualifying event &introduce yourself to the circle
‍Step 6 – Gain access to the online community &prepare for your first event
‍Step 7 – Meet in person &experience the benefits of BRX

How to get invited to BRX

All of the applicants we approve will be vetted and previously introduced to other members before joining.Therefore, referrals by existing BRX members will increase your chances of being accepted.If you do not have any referrals, make sure to show your most authentic self and communicate your intentions clearly.

Who is in BRX

BRX is tailored for the modern entrepreneur, therefore most of our members own e-commerce brands, agencies, or are creatives themselves.

The split is roughly 60% men &40% women.Approximately half of BRX 's members are 7-8 Figure entrepreneurs. The other half are high-potential individuals who are closely nurtured from within the circle.

Our circle currently holds members from fields such as:

E-commerce, various agencies, influencers, videography, photography, sales, finance, taxes, law and Saas.

Costs of BRX

Unlike other clubs, BRX Society has no membership fee. Pricing details for the annual qualifying trips are shared exclusively with selected candidates during the final stages of the application process. Do not book a call if you’re not ready to book a trip immediately after being accepted.

What does the cost cover

Depending on the location, all events usually include:
‍
- Luxury airport transfers
- Villa or penthouse stay
- Private chef
- Dedicated content creator for your personal brand
- Educational keynotes
- Exclusive experiences such as yacht charters, fitness events, massages, scuba diving, cultural excursions, and more
- BRX membership for 365 days, with access to the online community and app

What happens if I am accepted

Depending on availability within the club, you may get the chance to join BRX.
If this is the case, you will get the opportunity to solidify your membership by booking an event within 7 days of the official invitation.

Once booked, you will be onboarded by our team and will meet everyone.

What happens if I am rejected

If you are rejected, you will receive an email from us. Rejections are mostly either due to bad timing, as our club nears its full capacity at certain times, or it could be the lack of effort /skill displayed in your application. Therefore we will always encourage you to wait a few months, and re-send a new application.
